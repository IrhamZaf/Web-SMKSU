HISTORY OF SMK SAUJANA UTAMA

SMK Saujana Utama was opened on 22 May 2003. SMK Saujana Utama was named after its location which is located in the Saujana Utama housing area. SMKSU started operating in June 2003 (1 school session) with 65 students and 13 teachers. At that time there were only 2 classes namely; one form one class and one form 2 class. However, with the increase in the number of residents in the Saujana Utama housing area, the number of students in SMK Saujana Utama has also increased dramatically in 7 classes in 2004 and subsequent years. In 2015 the number of classes is expected to reach 80 classes. SMK Saujana Utama has an area of ​​12 acres (486 hectares). There are 7 buildings including classroom blocks, 1 administrative block and 1 block that houses the ICT lab (level 3) and the science lab (ground floor, 1 and 2). Apart from that, SMKSU is also equipped with various infrastructure as a facility for the implementation of all activities and educational programs conducted by school children, including halls and suraus. There is a security hut that houses 4 security guards who are on duty to monitor the safety of students and the environment of the school building.
